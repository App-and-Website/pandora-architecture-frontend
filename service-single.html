Our Proven Design and Approval Process

We guide homeowners, builders, and developers through every stage of the architectural design and approval process. Our proven approach ensures a seamless journey from concept design to approved plans, delivering quality, compliance, and confidence at every step.

Step 1

initial consultation

Discuss your ideas, project goals, budget, and site requirements so we can understand your vision and recommend the best approach.

Step 2

design & planning

We develop creative architectural designs and prepare detailed plans that balance functionality, aesthetics, and regulatory compliance.

Step 3

Approval & Documentation

We manage DA or CDC applications, prepares all required documentation, and coordinates with councils for a smooth approval process.

Step 4

Ready for Construction

Once approvals are secured, we deliver comprehensive construction-ready drawings to help bring your project to life with confidence.

Design & Approval Services We Provide

We specialise in CDC Subdivision Approvals, guiding clients through every stage with precision and care. Our subdivision expertise includes:

CDC Subdivision Approval

Our experienced team delivers innovative, practical, and compliant design solutions tailored to your project goals.

On-Site Detention (OSD) Preparation

Preparing and submitting OSD documentation, coordinating with all stakeholders, handling solicitor registration, and managing council lodgment.

Registration & Final Certification

Obtaining OSD registration, lodging documents with the PCA for the final CDC subdivision certificate, and managing Land Registry Services submission with follow-ups.
